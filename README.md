# 🛒 Flipkart Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

> **An interactive Sales Performance Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI to analyze Flipkart sales data, monitor business KPIs, evaluate product category performance, track monthly sales trends, understand customer purchasing behavior, and generate actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations and interactive reporting.**

---

## 📖 Project Description

The **Flipkart Sales Performance Dashboard** is a Business Intelligence solution developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to provide a comprehensive view of sales performance across multiple business dimensions. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights by visualizing sales trends, customer ratings, product categories, payment methods, and monthly performance.

Designed with an executive-friendly layout, the dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify high-performing product categories, evaluate customer preferences, and make informed business decisions using interactive reports and data-driven analytics.

This project demonstrates practical skills in **Power BI**, **Power Query**, **DAX**, **Data Modeling**, and **Data Visualization**, making it an ideal portfolio project for aspiring Data Analysts and Business Intelligence professionals.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze monthly sales trends
- Compare product category performance
- Evaluate customer ratings
- Understand payment method preferences
- Identify top-performing products
- Track product-wise sales contribution
- Support data-driven business decision-making

---

## 📊 Dashboard KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 💰 Total Sales | Overall revenue generated from all orders |
| 📦 Quantity Sold | Total quantity of products sold |
| ⭐ Customer Rating | Overall customer satisfaction score |
| 🛍 Total Orders | Total number of completed orders |

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Electronics generated the highest sales revenue.
- Customer ratings remained consistently positive across all major categories.
- Monthly sales trends reveal seasonal fluctuations in demand.
- Digital payment methods are preferred over traditional payment options.
- A small number of products contribute significantly to total sales.
- Product category analysis helps identify high-performing and underperforming segments.
- Interactive filtering enables detailed exploration of sales performance across categories.
